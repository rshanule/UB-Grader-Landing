    We are maintaining this live demo for the purposes of allowing the graders of CSE 442 to easily access our app in a non-technical way, as per the requirements. This live demo doesn't actually make sense for an official release, because our software is meant to be independently deployed by each institution that wants to use it. For the purposes of grading, we provide this link to a live demo as well as 3 sample accounts for you to use.


    Accounts and Login

    We do not have a traditional account creation system. This software is designed for a school-like heirarchy, as such we have three essential types of users: Admins, Instructors, and Students.

    We currently use Google Auth for logins. You may log in via your own Google Account, but you will not be enrolled in any courses or have any privileges. For the purposes of testing, we provide you with three sample accounts.


    Admins

    Admins are the highest power in the land. These accounts must be manually put into place by the school. Admins have access to everything. Most importantly, they have the exclusive power to create courses and assign instructors to those courses.

    Instructors

    Instructors are non-admin users who have been given instructor privileges for one or more courses. Instructors have the power to create categories, assignments, and add students to their courses. They do not have special powers outside of their assigned courses.

    Sample Account:

    Password: cse442fall2017


    Students

    Students are the common user. They see only the courses in which they have been enrolled. They will recieve assignments through those courses, submit their work to the AutoGrader, and receive scores.
